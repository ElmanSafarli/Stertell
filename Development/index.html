Frequently Asked Questions

Questions? Look here

What is SterTell?

SterTell is a global telecom company that empowers mobile operators and enterprises with the tools to deliver messages securely, detect fraud, and optimize traffic to drive maximum revenue.

How does SterTell ensure SMS delivery success?

We use advanced routing and real-time monitoring to maximize SMS delivery rates and avoid spam traps.

Can SterTell help prevent A2P traffic leakage?

Yes, our intelligent firewall and analytics tools help operators detect and block unauthorized A2P routes.

What industries benefit most from SterTell solutions?

Telecoms, financial services, e-commerce, and governments rely on SterTell for secure and reliable messaging.

Is SterTell compliant with telecom regulations?

Absolutely. SterTell complies with international telecom standards including GDPR, ISO, and local regulations.

Do you offer custom messaging or firewall solutions?

Yes, we build custom solutions tailored to your infrastructure, whether it's messaging, fraud detection, or routing.
