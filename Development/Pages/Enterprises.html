Enterprises — Multi-Channel Messaging Services

SterTell offers a robust, high-performance, and user-friendly multi-channel messaging platform designed to manage global communication flows across SMS, Telegram, Viber, WhatsApp, and more.

Scalable Messaging for the Enterprise

Powered by a comprehensive mobile delivery network, our platform easily handles any message volume while ensuring reliability and scalability.

Network Protection

Protect telecom infrastructure from overload and congestion using intelligent traffic management.

Monetize A2P SMS

Capture value from incoming application-to-person messages through targeted monetization tools.

Fraud Prevention

Prevent telecom fraud and abuse with real-time detection and control systems built into the platform.

Targeted SMS Marketing

Send the right message to the right people at the right time and price.

  • Leverage your database for personalized SMS engagement
  • Deliver trigger-based messages for timely conversion
  • Boost retention with automated communication flows
  • Maximize ROI through performance-driven segmentation

SterTell SMS Campaign Portal

Multi-Channel Messaging: Send SMS, Telegram, Viber, and WhatsApp messages in one place
Flexible Providers: Select your preferred messaging vendors per channel
Powerful Segmentation: Target specific groups with dynamic filters
Campaign Analytics: Track opens, delivery, and engagement across channels

Whether you're a small business or global brand, our portal helps you manage every message with insight and efficiency.

  • Sign in to your account
