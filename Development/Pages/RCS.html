New Projects — RCS (Rich Communication Services)

SterTell’s RCS platform provides telecom operators with a cutting-edge, modular toolkit designed to deliver the next generation of rich, interactive messaging services.

Expansive, Flexible, and Future-Ready

With RCS, you can offer your customers a seamless, engaging communication experience — combining SMS reliability with rich media, interactive features, and business messaging capabilities.

Modular Architecture

Built to scale across diverse environments and easily customized to operator needs.

Seamless Integration

Integrates smoothly with existing telecom infrastructure and supports multi-channel messaging.

Rapid Feature Rollout

Accelerates deployment of new RCS services and upgrades without delay.

Operator-Friendly by Design

SterTell prioritizes operators’ needs by helping them:

  • Accelerate time-to-market for new services
  • Maximize monetization opportunities with rich business messaging
  • Simplify orchestration and management of RCS campaigns
  • Deliver rich content, chatbots, and interactive engagement

Result: Stronger engagement. New revenue streams. Competitive edge.

What RCS Enables

Rich Media Messaging: Send images, videos, carousels, and branded templates
Business Messaging: Enable branded communication at scale with verified sender info
Real-Time Indicators: Typing indicators, read receipts, delivery confirmations
AI-Powered Bots: Use chatbots and smart replies for dynamic, two-way conversations

SterTell equips telecom operators to lead the shift to next-gen messaging through advanced RCS capabilities.
